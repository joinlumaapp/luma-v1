# LUMA Vision

## Mission
LUMA is built to redefine how people meet, connect, and build meaningful relationships in the digital age.

## What LUMA Is
LUMA is a **compatibility-first social discovery platform** that combines:
- **Algorithmic matching** — Uyum Analizi (20-question compatibility test) calculates real compatibility between users
- **Social content** — Instagram-like feed with stories, posts (photo/video/text), and social interactions
- **Live discovery** — Omegle-style random video matching for instant face-to-face connections
- **Smart recommendations** — AI-powered daily match suggestions and mutual place recommendations

## Core Philosophy
**"Gerçek uyum için kendin ol."**

Every feature in LUMA serves one goal: helping users find genuinely compatible people. Unlike apps that rely on looks alone, LUMA shows users how compatible they truly are — through scientifically designed personality and compatibility analysis.

## What Makes LUMA Different
1. **Uyum Yüzdesi** — Every profile shows a compatibility score. Users know before matching how well they'd get along. This is LUMA's signature feature.
2. **3 Ways to Discover** — Akış (social feed), Keşfet (card swipe), Canlı (live video). Three different entry points that all lead to meaningful connections.
3. **Not Just Dating** — LUMA supports multiple goals: marriage, relationships, friendships, cultural exchange, travel companions. The Hedefim (My Goal) system lets users declare their intention transparently.
4. **Gamification & Retention** — Kaşif missions, weekly leaderboards, profile strength, mood status — features that keep users engaged daily, like a social network, not just a dating app.
5. **Fair Monetization** — No feature is locked. Every user can try every feature. Packages only increase quantities and remove limits. This creates desire, not frustration.

## Target Audience
- Primary: 18-35 year olds in Turkey
- Secondary: Turkish-speaking users worldwide
- People seeking genuine connections, not superficial swipes
- Users who value knowing compatibility before investing time

## Long-Term Vision
LUMA aims to become Turkey's most popular social discovery app — a platform where people naturally come to meet others, share moments, and build relationships based on real compatibility. Think Instagram meets Tinder meets Omegle, powered by a compatibility engine.

## Design Philosophy
- Modern, polished UI with smooth animations (Bumpy reference)
- Gradient color schemes (pink-peach for light, purple-dark for premium screens)
- Micro-animations on every interaction
- Warm, inviting aesthetic that feels premium yet accessible
