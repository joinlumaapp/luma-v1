# LUMA Vision

## Mission
LUMA exists to transform how people discover, connect, and build meaningful relationships in the digital age. Not just romantic ones -- friendships, cultural exchanges, travel companions, and everything in between.

## What LUMA Is
LUMA is a **compatibility-first social discovery platform** -- a hybrid of Instagram, Tinder, and Omegle, powered by a proprietary compatibility engine.

- **Instagram gibi Akis** -- A social feed with stories, posts (photo/video/text), likes, comments, and follows. Users share moments and build an audience.
- **Tinder gibi Kesfet** -- Card-based swipe discovery with compatibility scores front and center. Swipe right, swipe left, or send a Super Begeni -- but every card shows how compatible you truly are.
- **Omegle gibi Canli** -- Random video matching for instant face-to-face connections. No filters, no games -- just two people discovering each other live.
- **Eslesmeler + Mesajlar** -- A unified hub for matches, messages, followers, likes received, and profile viewers.
- **Profil** -- Profile management, settings, Kasif missions, gamification, and personal stats.

LUMA is not one thing. It is three discovery engines wrapped in a social layer, unified by a single idea: **compatibility matters more than looks**.

## Core Philosophy
**"Gercek uyum icin kendin ol."**

Every feature in LUMA serves one goal: helping users find genuinely compatible people. Unlike apps that rely on appearance alone, LUMA shows users how compatible they truly are -- through a scientifically designed 20-question Uyum Analizi that produces a real compatibility percentage (47-97 range) between any two users. 90+ = Super Uyum with special UI treatment.

## 4 Relationship Types

LUMA supports four distinct relationship types, each with its own discovery path:

1. **Takip** -- One-way follow (from Akis, Profil, or Canli). Like Instagram -- follow someone to see their content.
2. **Arkadas** -- Mutual follow. When two people follow each other, they become friends. Opens DM and additional interactions.
3. **Eslesme** -- Mutual like from Kesfet. The classic match -- both users swiped right. Triggers the konfeti/kalp animation.
4. **Super Begeni** -- Jeton-powered special like. Sends a highlighted notification and jumps to the top of the other user's queue.

## Not Just Dating

LUMA explicitly supports multiple relationship goals through the **Hedefim** (My Goal) system. Users select from 5 goals:

- **Evlenmek** (Marriage)
- **Iliski bulmak** (Find a relationship)
- **Sohbet/Arkadas** (Chat / Friendship)
- **Kulturleri ogrenmek** (Learn cultures)
- **Dunyayi gezmek** (Travel the world)

This is not a dating-only app. A user looking for travel companions or same-gender friendships is just as welcome as someone seeking a life partner. The compatibility engine works across all goals.

## Jeton Economy

Jeton is the universal in-app currency powering special interactions across all tabs:

- **Selam Gonder** -- Send an intro message before matching
- **Super Begeni** -- Highlighted like with priority notification
- **Boost** -- 24 saat boyunca profili one cikar (10x gorunurluk)
- **Canli** -- Video sessions for free users
- **Sesli/Goruntulu Arama** -- Voice/video calls for free users

Jeton can be purchased (79,99 TL / 199,99 TL for 500 / 349,99 TL for 1000) or earned through Kasif missions and watching rewarded ads.

## Compatibility Engine

The heart of LUMA is the **Uyum Analizi** -- a 20-question test across 8 psychological categories. Every user takes it during onboarding. The engine calculates a compatibility percentage (47-97) between any two users.

This score appears everywhere:
- On every Kesfet card
- On every profile visit
- In Gunun Eslesmesi recommendations
- In Canli match suggestions
- 90+ = **Super Uyum** badge with glow animation

An optional 5-question **Kisilik Testi** adds profile tags like "Acik Fikirli" or "Macerasever" for additional discovery signals. It has zero effect on the compatibility algorithm.

## Design Philosophy

- **Bumpy-inspired premium animations** -- Smooth, polished micro-interactions on every action (like, follow, match, super like)
- **Gradient color schemes** -- Pink-peach for light mode, purple-dark for premium screens
- **Match animation** -- Konfeti and kalp explosion when two users match
- **Warm, inviting aesthetic** -- Premium feeling yet accessible to everyone
- **Turkish-first UI** -- All user-facing text in Turkish, culturally relevant design patterns

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: 18-35 year olds in Turkey
- **Secondary**: Turkish-speaking users worldwide
- **Tertiary**: Global expansion after Turkey market is established
- People seeking genuine connections -- romantic, platonic, or social
- Users who value knowing compatibility before investing time

## Turkey First, Then Global

LUMA launches in Turkey. The compatibility engine, UI language, cultural references, and monetization are all optimized for the Turkish market. Once product-market fit is proven:

1. Turkish-speaking diaspora (Germany, Netherlands, UK, USA)
2. MENA region (cultural proximity)
3. Global launch with localization

## Retention-First Design

LUMA is built for daily engagement, not one-time downloads:

- **Kasif Missions** -- Daily tasks that reward jeton
- **Weekly Uyum Raporu** -- Personalized compatibility insights delivered every week
- **Mood Status** -- Real-time mood sharing that refreshes social presence
- **Buz Kirici Oyunlar** -- Ice-breaker games that make first messages easy
- **Gunun Eslesmesi** -- AI-powered daily match suggestion that brings users back
- **Ortak Mekan Onerisi** -- Post-match place suggestions that move connections offline
- **Bu Haftanin Yildizlari** -- Weekly leaderboard that creates healthy competition
- **Story and Post Feed** -- Instagram-like content that makes LUMA a daily habit, not just a dating tool
