# LUMA - IARC Content Rating Questionnaire Answers

This document contains the answers for the Google Play IARC content rating questionnaire. These answers determine the app's content rating across different rating systems (PEGI, ESRB, USK, etc.).

**Expected Rating**: Mature 17+ / PEGI 18

---

| Does the app contain graphic violence? | No |
| Does the app contain cartoon or fantasy violence? | No |
| Does the app contain realistic violence? | No |
| Does the app depict violence against specific groups? | No |

## Section 2: Sexuality

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Does the app contain sexual content? | No |
| Does the app contain nudity? | No |
| Does the app contain sexual themes? | Yes (dating context) |
| Does the app allow sexual content in user-generated content? | No (prohibited by Terms of Service) |
| Is the app a dating or social networking app? | Yes |

**Notes**: LUMA is a dating app. While no sexual content is provided by the app itself, the nature of dating apps means users may discuss romantic or sexual topics in private messages. Selfie verification and content moderation are in place to prevent inappropriate content.

## Summary

| Rating System | Expected Rating |
|---|---|

| PEGI | 18 |
| ESRB | Mature 17+ |
| USK | 18 |
| ClassInd | 18 |
| GRAC | 18 |
| ACB | MA 15+ |

**Primary rating factors**:
- Dating app (social/romantic interaction between adults)
- User-generated content (messages, profiles)
- Age restriction requirement (18+)

**Mitigating factors**:
- No violent content
- No sexual content provided by app
- No ads
- Content moderation and reporting system in place
- Selfie verification for user authenticity
- Prohibited content policy with enforcement
